Brand Guidelines

When using Grove's brand assets, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Do not alter, rotate, or modify the logo
  • Maintain clear space around the logo
  • Do not use the logo on busy backgrounds that reduce legibility
  • Do not change the colors of the logo
